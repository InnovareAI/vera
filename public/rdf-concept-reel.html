Concept Reel · May 2026

RDF · A wearable state of calm

Four films on returning
to yourself.

The modern world overstimulates. RDF exists to reduce that — not by escaping reality, but by helping you stay stable within it.

This reel explores the brand's regulation system through four visual ideas: the dragonfly that pauses time, the breath that finds its rhythm, the touch that brings you home, and the second skin that holds you.

01 · Pause

The dragonfly that stops time.

A red dragonfly enters frame, lands on white, flutters its wings, and lifts away. A brief moment of reflection — a reminder that life moves quickly, and sometimes we need to slow down.

Idea · Beauty, intrigue, presence· Brand role · Logo film· 8s · 16:9

02 · Breath

A breath that finds its rhythm.

Lower face only — nose, lips, and neck. A woman breathes deeply against a cinematic natural background. Through breath, the brand speaks calm before it ever speaks words.

Idea · Communicate ease, build intrigue· Brand role · State setter· 8s · 16:9

03 · Touch

Two hands, one return.

Hands and fingers softly meeting. The brand's core mechanic — the sensory anchor in the Reset Hoodie pocket — translated into pure visual feeling. Through touch, you return.

Idea · Make the sensory experience visible· Brand role · Anchor reveal· 8s · 16:9

04 · Second Skin

The fabric, made flesh.

A woman covered in red dragonfly scales, gently embracing herself. The Tee Second Skin as concept — the garment that disappears into you. Sneak peek for the product line.

Idea · Product as second skin· Brand role · Tee teaser· 8s · 16:9
